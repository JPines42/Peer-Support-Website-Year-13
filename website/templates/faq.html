FAQ

When does Peer Support start?

Peer Support takes place every Homeroom on Fridays from 8:45-9:00.

Where does Peer Support happen?

Peer Support happens within the Year 7 and 8 Homeroom classes. Peer Support may also use the surrounding area within SMC.

Who looks after the Year 7 and 8s?

The Peer Support Leaders will look after them until Period 1 starts. Please note that no teachers will be watching them.

What is expected to be done at Peer Support?

There will be a range of activities that the Peer Support Leaders could decide on for the juniors to engage in. E.g. Teamwork building, competitive exercises.

Will there be a Sing-off or Dance-off this year?

Yes! In Term 3 the Peer Support competitions will be held in the Auditorium.

What do you need to bring to Peer Support?

You won’t have to bring anything most times, as the Peer Support Leaders come prepared with everything needed for that day’s session. Sometimes a pencil or a device will be useful.
